Why use a tool like Prism?

Built for transparent product makers & and teams who crave feedback, prism is not only good for capturing bugs and screenshots of product designs. We’ve built it to allow for anyone to quickly & share their own unique perspective in a more visual way.

How do i get started using Prism?

Simply sign up here to create an account.
 We’ve also built our own Prism Lens iOS app and Google Chrome web extension for snapping and sharing screenshots straight to prism in a jiffy.

What platforms does Prism integrate with?

Prism integrates nicely with most of the popular communication tools you are already using today like slack, trello, jira & over email as well as social platforms like twitter, pinterest and facebook.
